here for some great guitarists in rock history

Jimi Hendrix

 is an American guitarist, songwriter and singer, founder of the Anglo-American group The Jimi Hendrix Experience, active from 1966 to 1970. Despite an international career that lasted only four years, he is considered one of the greatest electric guitar players and one of the most important musicians of the 20th century. In August 2009, Time Magazine ranked him first in its list of the 10 greatest electric guitar players of all time.

Frank Anthony Iommi

known as Tony, is a British guitarist born February 19, 1948 in Birmingham, England, famous for being one of the founding members of the heavy metal band Black Sabbath, of which he is the only permanent member. Iommi is recognized as one of the most influential guitarists in heavy metal. In 2012, Gibson put him at the top of its list of the ten greatest metal guitarists of all time.

Ritchie Blackmore

(Richard Hugh Blackmore), born April 14, 1945 in Weston-super-Mare, is a British guitarist, best known for having once belonged to the groups Deep Purple, Rainbow, and for being the founder of Blackmore's Night, including his wife (Candice Night) and he are the only permanent members. Eric Clapton

born March 30, 1945 in Ripley, near Guildford, is a British blues rock guitarist, singer, songwriter and performer. In 2011, in its ranking "the 100 greatest guitarists of all time", the monthly Rolling Stone placed him in second position, just behind Jimi Hendrix.

Jimmy Page

James Patrick Page, known as Jimmy Page, is a rock guitarist, producer and composer, born on January 9, 1944 in Heston in the suburbs of London. He is the founder, leader and composer of the majority of the original repertoire of the famous rock band Led Zeppelin.

Andrew Latimer

born May 17, 1949 in Guildford, Surrey, UK is a British guitarist and singer-songwriter. He is one of the founding members of the progressive rock band Camel, along with Peter Bardens and Andy Ward..

Rory Gallagher

born March 2, 1948 in Ballyshannon, Ireland and died June 14, 1995 in London, is an Irish blues rock guitarist, singer, composer and producer, known for being the founder of the group Taste and for his solo career.

Jeff beck

Geoffrey Arnold Beck said Jeff Beck, born in London on June 24, 1944, is a British rock guitarist. He is one of three guitarists, along with Eric Clapton and Jimmy Page, to have played in the group The Yardbirds in the 1960s

Robin Trower

Robin Leonard Trower (born March 9, 1945) is an English rock guitarist who found success with Procol Harum during the 1960s, then as the leader of his own trio (though not the vocalist).
